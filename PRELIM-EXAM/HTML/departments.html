Our Departments

The City Government of Bacoor is committed to providing excellent services to its citizens through various specialized departments. Explore the departments below to know their roles and how they can assist you.

  • Mayor's Office
    Oversees city operations, implements policies, and manages public administration.
    Contact: mayor@bacoor.gov.ph | +63 123 456 7890
  • Health Office
    Provides public health services, medical programs, and health education.
    Contact: health@bacoor.gov.ph | +63 123 456 7891
  • Engineering Office
    Handles city infrastructure, road maintenance, and public construction projects.
    Contact: engineering@bacoor.gov.ph | +63 123 456 7892
  • Social Welfare Office
    Supports vulnerable populations through social programs, aid, and community development.
    Contact: socialwelfare@bacoor.gov.ph | +63 123 456 7893
  • Tourism Office
    Promotes local tourism, organizes events, and enhances the city’s cultural attractions.
    Contact: tourism@bacoor.gov.ph | +63 123 456 7894
