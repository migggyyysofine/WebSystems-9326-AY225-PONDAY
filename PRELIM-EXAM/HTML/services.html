Our Services

The City Government of Bacoor provides a variety of services to support citizens, businesses, and community needs. Use the form below to submit requests or inquiries about our services.

Available Services

  • Business Permit – Apply for or renew your business permits to legally operate in the city.
  • Barangay Clearance – Request clearances needed for residency, employment, or other official transactions.
  • Health Services – Access medical check-ups, vaccinations, and other public health programs.
  • Social Assistance – Aid programs for families and individuals in need, including livelihood support.
  • Tourism Assistance – Information and assistance for local tourism activities and events.
