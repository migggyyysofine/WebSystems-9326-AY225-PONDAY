History

Bacoor City is one of the oldest towns in Cavite, with a rich history dating back to the Spanish colonial period. It was known as a hub of commerce and culture, playing an important role in the Philippine Revolution. Today, Bacoor combines its historical heritage with modern urban development.

Vision

Bacoor City envisions a progressive, inclusive, and sustainable community where every citizen has access to quality services and opportunities for growth.

Mission

The city government of Bacoor is committed to delivering efficient public services with transparency, integrity, and citizen-focused governance. It aims to foster economic growth, social welfare, and environmental sustainability.

Culture & Heritage

Bacoor is known for its vibrant local festivals, traditional cuisine, and strong community spirit. The city preserves its cultural heritage through historical landmarks, museums, and local arts programs.

Economy

The city's economy thrives on trade, services, and small-to-medium enterprises. Bacoor is strategically located near Metro Manila, making it a center for commerce and residential growth.

Community Engagement

Bacoor City prioritizes citizen participation through local programs, outreach activities, and initiatives that strengthen community ties and promote public welfare.
